# Code Of Conduct

This project uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- using welcoming and inclusive language;
- respecting differing viewpoints and experiences;
- accepting constructive criticism gracefully;
- focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory jokes and language;
- publishing others' private information without explicit permission;
- sustained disruption of project discussion;
- other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and
pull requests that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also
temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate,
threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported privately to the repository owner.
